## Executive Summary

This report identifies **35+ grant opportunities** for NGEOA's 8-week heavy equipment operator training program, organized by deadline urgency. NGEOA qualifies as a nonprofit under New Life City Center church, serves opportunity youth (ages 18-24), provides OSHA 10 certification, and operates in Miami-Dade County's Goulds neighborhood.

**Quick Stats:**
- **14 grants** with deadlines in next 90 days (HIGH PRIORITY)
- **8 grants** with rolling/continuous deadlines
- **13 corporate/foundation** opportunities (invitation or competitive)
- **Total potential funding:** $500K - $2M+ available
