## Recommendation for NGEOA

### Best Option: CareerSafe Online ($25-37/student)

**Why:**
- Lowest cost with educational/nonprofit pricing
- Official DOL card ships in 2 days (fastest)
- OSHA-authorized provider
- Bulk enrollment discounts
- Used by NCCER programs

**Action Steps:**
1. Contact CareerSafe: 888.614.7233 or orders@careersafeonline.com
2. Request nonprofit/educational pricing quote
3. Ask about bulk enrollment (10+ students)
4. Set up organizational account

### Alternative: Become an OSHA Outreach Trainer (Year 2+)

**If training 50+ students/year, consider in-house delivery:**

**Requirements:**
1. Complete OSHA #510 (Construction Safety Standards) - ~$845
2. Have 5 years construction safety experience (or degree + 3 years)
3. Complete OSHA #500 Trainer Course (4 days) - ~$845

**2026 Florida Training Dates:**
- March 23-26: Jacksonville
- June 8-11: Wesley Chapel
- June 22-25: Orlando
- August 24-27: Fort Lauderdale

**Break-even:** ~30 students at $50/student savings

## Time Required

- **Course Duration:** 10+ hours
- **Minimum Days:** 2 calendar days (OSHA requires no more than 7.5 hours/day)
- **Online Completion Window:** 180 days from enrollment
- **Pass Requirement:** 70% on final exam (3 attempts allowed)
