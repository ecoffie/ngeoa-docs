## Partnership Approach

### Letter of Support Template Request

When reaching out, request:
1. **Letter of Support** for grant applications (CareerSource, DOL)
2. **Hiring commitment** - willing to interview graduates
3. **Job shadow opportunity** - 1-2 students during training
4. **Equipment access** - possible donation of machine time

### Talking Points

1. **Free trained operators** - No cost to hire, grant-funded training
2. **OSHA 10 certified** - Safety-trained workforce
3. **Local pipeline** - Goulds/Homestead area residents
4. **Ongoing relationship** - Multiple cohorts per year
5. **No obligation** - Just agree to interview graduates

### Sample Outreach Email

```
Subject: Free Heavy Equipment Operator Training Pipeline - Partnership Opportunity

Hi [Name],

NGEOA Academy is launching a grant-funded heavy equipment operator training program in the Goulds area of Miami. We're partnering with New Life City Center and preparing for CareerSource WIOA approval.

We're looking for employers who would be willing to:
- Interview our graduates (no hiring obligation)
- Provide a Letter of Support for our grant applications
- Optionally: offer job shadow days for students

Our graduates will be:
- OSHA 10 certified
- Trained on excavators, loaders, backhoes, and skid steers
- Ready to work in 8 weeks

Would you have 15 minutes to discuss a partnership? We're building relationships now for our first cohort.

Best regards,
[Name]
NGEOA Academy
ngeoacademy.org
```
