# Roadmap & Budget Framework: Florida Church Network (3–5 Years)

**Companion to:** `PRD-Workforce-Academy.md`, `EXECUTION-PLAN.md`  
**Intent:** Move from **pilot** → **five active locations** → **scaled engagement across 70+ churches**, with **budget and benchmarks** tied to gates.

**Status (discovery):** **Pilot only** — Goulds (Miami). **Five host-church locations** and **70+ church network** are **roadmap placeholders**, not active workstreams, until the pilot clears **go/no-go** criteria in `RESEARCH-GATES-AND-GO-NO-GO.md`. **Budget and revenue:** **unknown** — research phase; **volunteer** labor until funded.

---

## 1. Scale thesis

- **Pilot:** Prove **one** site can hit enrollment, safety, placement, and data discipline.  
- **Replication:** Standardize **playbook + dashboard + QC** so site 2–5 are **clones with local customization** (employers, pastors, county workforce).  
- **Network:** Most of **70+ Florida churches** do not need to run equipment yards—many will **refer**, **host info sessions**, **provide volunteers**, or **connect employers**. Only a subset become **full training sites**.

---

## 2. Church partner tiers (define MOU for each)

| Tier | Role | Minimum commitment | Data you track |
|------|------|--------------------|----------------|
| **T1 — Host site** | Primary facility; local PM or site lead | Space, schedule, insurance alignment | Full site KPIs |
| **T2 — Training partner** | Cohort intake assistance, member referrals | Quarterly referral target | Referrals → enrollments |
| **T3 — Employer connector** | Business relationships, job fairs | Intro meetings / year | Interviews, hires |
| **T4 — Prayer / awareness** | Communications only | None numeric | Optional attendance at events |

**Rule:** Expand **T1 sites** only when **PMO + QC** from prior tier is green for **two consecutive quarters**.

## 3. Year-by-year roadmap (indicative)

Adjust fiscal year start to your accounting calendar.

### Year 1 — Prove & document

| Quarter | Milestones |
|---------|------------|
| Q1 | P0–P1 complete; pilot cohort running; dashboard v1; employer pipeline live |
| Q2 | First cohort completion; placement proof; audit QC; budget actuals |
| Q3 | Playbook v1; identify site 2 lead + preliminary MOU |
| Q4 | Site 2 launch **or** defer with documented reasons; funder renewal package |

**Exit Year 1:** ≥1 site meeting placement benchmark; playbook; clean books.

### Year 2 — Replicate

- **Site 2** fully operational; **Site 3** in late Y2 or early Y3 depending on cash and talent.  
- Central **part-time PMO** role (could be fractional ED or ops) for standards and reporting.  
- **15–25** churches engaged at T2–T4 (referral/awareness).

### Year 3 — Five locations

- **Five T1 (or equivalent capacity)** sites **or** fewer T1 sites with **higher cohort throughput**—choose one model and state it in board resolution.  
- **Network:** 30–50 churches in T2–T4.

### Years 4–5 — Network effects & sustainability

- **70+ church** touchpoints: mapping which are T1 vs referral.  
- Endowment / recurring employer fees / workforce contracts mix—**target: <50% single-source dependency** for central ops.  
- Consider **regional hub** (equipment + instructor pool) vs fully distributed sites—**economic model decision**.

---

## 4. Expansion criteria (go / no-go)

**Go to next site when:**

- Prior site: green on **safety**, **placement**, **budget variance**, **data completeness** for **2 quarters**.  
- **Named** site lead + PM + instructor pipeline identified.  
- **Employer** pipeline in that MSA not fully overlapping (or shared capacity documented).  
- **Cash runway** ≥6 months central + site incremental costs.

**No-go examples:** unresolved OSHA-style incident; grant clawback risk; leadership turnover without succession.

## 7. Risks to multi-site scale

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Quality dilution | PMO audits; instructor certification; pause expansion |
| Competing visions across pastors | Single statewide playbook + local addendum |
| Funding fragmentation | Unified CRM and grant calendar |
| Equipment sharing failure | Hub model or rental SLAs |
