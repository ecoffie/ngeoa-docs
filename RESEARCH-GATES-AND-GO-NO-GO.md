## 1. Instructor credentials & curriculum length (Florida context)

### What to verify

- **Instructional model:** Will the church deliver **NCCER-aligned** training, **partner** with an accredited/registered provider, or **hybrid** (classroom at church + hands-on at partner yard)?
- **NCCER:** Industry-standard construction/heavy equipment credentials are often delivered through **NCCER**-based programs; curriculum updates are published by [NCCER Heavy Equipment Operations](https://www.nccer.org/workforce-development-programs/disciplines/craft-details/heavy-equipment-operators). Total recommended hours vary by credential path (example: multi-level curriculum on the order of **hundreds of hours** — confirm against the edition you adopt).
- **Florida regulation:** If the program charges tuition or meets definitions of **private postsecondary** education, **Commission for Independent Education (CIE)** or other rules may apply — **board + Florida counsel** should confirm before taking fees or issuing certificates beyond employer partnership.
- **Earl’s role:** Document actual licenses (e.g. **CDL** if relevant), **NCCER** (if any), **OSHA** outreach (if applicable), and **train-the-trainer** status. Gap-fill plan: see `EARL-CREDENTIAL-AUDIT.md`.

## 4. CareerSource South Florida & workforce funding

### Public starting points (verify before relying on grants)

- [CareerSource South Florida](https://www.careersourcesfl.com/) — regional workforce board; **training** and **WIOA** processes are county- and eligibility-dependent.
- **Eligible Training Provider List (ETPL):** WIOA-funded training generally runs through **approved providers** and **targeted occupations** — a **new** church-run program may need **partnership** with an ETPL institution or a separate approval path. Do **not** promise participants WIOA funding until CSSF confirms steps.
- **Action:** One **intake meeting** with CSSF (use official contact from their site — PDF phone lists in old docs may be stale) and document **next steps** and **eligible occupations** for your ZIP/county.

### Output

- Meeting notes: **contact name**, **requirements**, **timeline**, **occupation codes** if provided.
